# Comprehensive Wellness Platform - Complete Feature Documentation

## Overview
This document outlines all features, functionalities, and capabilities for a complete wellness platform that combines sleep tracking, health monitoring, hydration management, fitness training, mental wellness, and nutrition tracking into one unified experience.

## 1. SLEEP TRACKING & MANAGEMENT

### Core Sleep Features
- **AI-Powered Sleep Tracking**: Audio-based sleep analysis using machine learning
- **Sleep Cycle Detection**: Automatic identification of light, deep, REM, and awake phases
- **Sleep Quality Score**: Personalized rating based on duration and quality
- **Sleep Duration Tracking**: Hours slept per night
- **Bedtime & Wake Time Logging**: Track sleep schedule
- **Sleep Pattern Analysis**: Weekly and monthly trends
- **90th Percentile Accuracy**: High-precision tracking without wearables

### Sleep Audio Features
- **Snoring Detection**: Record and analyze snoring patterns
- **Sleep Talk Recording**: Capture nighttime vocalizations
- **Playback Feature**: Review recorded audio
- **Sleep Apnea Detection**: Early warning signals
- **Ambient Noise Monitoring**: Environmental sound levels

### Sleep Aid Content
- **100+ Sleep Sounds**: Extensive audio library
  - White noise, brown noise, pink noise, green noise
  - Rain, ocean, thunder, wind, forest sounds
  - ASMR audio tracks
  - Nature ambiance (birds, water, campfire)
  - Clock ticking, fan sounds
- **Customizable Sound Mixer**: Combine multiple sounds with individual volume controls
- **Bedtime Stories**: Adult and children's narratives for relaxation
- **Sleep Meditations**: Guided sessions for falling asleep
- **Relaxing Music**: Instrumental tracks designed for sleep

### Smart Wake Features
- **Smart Alarm Clock**: Wakes during lightest sleep phase
- **Customizable Wake Window**: Set time range for optimal wake-up
- **Gentle Wake-Up**: Gradual alarm intensity
- **Natural Rhythm Sync**: Aligns with body's sleep cycles

### Sleep Analytics
- **Sleep Reports**: Daily, weekly, monthly summaries
- **Sleep Score After 5 Nights**: Personalized baseline
- **Comparison Data**: See how you compare to other users
- **Trend Visualization**: Charts and graphs
- **Sleep Diary**: Manual notes and observations
- **Pattern Recognition**: Identify sleep disruptors
